Focused Learning is What Matters

Posted by Jeya Karthika on June 22nd, 2013.

Web design and development is a vast field which evolves very quickly. For someone like me to make this the career path, I would suggest to stay focused. I know html5, css3, jQuery and javascript. I know photoshop, illustrator and indesign.

I know everything but feels like nothing because during the process of learning it is quite easy to lose focus. It is easy to keep on learning but the most difficult part is knowing the difference between what you know and what you think you know. I started with html5 but before completely knowing everything I moved in to css3, then javascript and before completing that jQuery. This just makes me the Jack of all trades and king of none.

So, I have made a decision to stick to one at a time and keep to it until am completely confident with it. I have realized that focused learning is what matters the most.
