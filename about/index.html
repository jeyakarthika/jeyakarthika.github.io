My Story

No, it was not my music band.

It was my wedding & I was trying to make a beautiful website for it. That's when it happened.

Does love happen that fast? Well, It happened to me. I just fell in love with crafting beautiful & unique user experiences.

It shouldn't have been a surprise. The symptoms were always there. An unexplainable obsession with colours, a passion to make things happen, an inability to do anything short of perfection and a love for making kick-ass presentations.

So hey, Here I am, to make your ideas happen.

Who am I?

About Me, I am Jeya Karthika, SharePoint Systems Engineer at Dimension Data. I ♥ crafting colourful pixels and writing clean markups. Oh and, I ♥ being me. ◕◡◕

What am I doing here?

Apart from my Crazy Thoughts, I will be sharing my learning outcomes, some great web design/development resources and inspiration that captures my attention.

How to get in touch?

I am always interested in hearing about new challenges or side-projects & would love to hear from you. Email me or just tweet me below.
