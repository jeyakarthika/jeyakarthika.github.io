Posted by Jeya Karthika on November 26th, 2013.



Jeya Karthika's photostream on Flickr.

These photographs account for my month long absence from blogging... So, I was in India for a month spending some quality time with friends and family. I didn't have much time for learning web development stuff  while I was there. But, I managed to do 2 things while I was away.

  1. Come up with some concept projects to work on after the vacation which I hope will help shape my learning in a positive manner.
  2. Make a  list of the resources I have been using for my reference and now available for your use here. Do you think I have missed out an awesome resource that myself and others could benefit from? Email me or tweet me the link and I ‘ll add it here ♥ :)

On my learning front, Am starting a course with Thinkful from Dec 3rd.. Further updates on that will follow.. Until then, Hope you will enjoy what I saw through my lens in flickr :) For those who are interested, I used a Nikon D5100 with a combination of Nikkor 50mm and Tamron 18-270mm.

Do let me know if you like my photos :)
